This is a repository, and a sort of stable space, for information I hold dear. My discomfort comes inherent to things such as bitrot, forgotten archival, malicious data destruction... The Uncontrollable Decay of Data. A safety net came in the form of this site. Simple HTML pasted to a file, stored on an SSD in the ether. Forever to roam until death, or lack of hosting.
I run this site in hopes to spread information to work with these items, and keep my life forever tied to the unthinking, cold webs that we call the Internet.

AgalmicAutomata is a joinable group, rather than my own """brand""" identity. It is an open source unified identity, one that can hopefully outlast me or my willingness to maintain the group. As for the name and icon, this is to create a "symbol" for things made under the philosophy of being open source, not limited to manufacturer or supply chain logistics.

I have a Discord, being that you wish to randomly contact me.
