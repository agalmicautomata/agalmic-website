This is a repository, and a sort of stable space, for information I hold dear. My discomfort comes inherent to things such as bitrot, forgotten archival, malicious data destruction... The Uncontrollable Decay of Data. A safety net came in the form of this site. Simple HTML pasted to a file, stored on an SSD in the ether. Forever to roam until death, or lack of hosting.
My name is Aternalogic, meaning "Forever Logical", homage to the now ruling logic processors that dominate our humble home. I run this site in hopes to spread information to work with these items, and keep my life forever tied to the unthinking, cold webs that we call the Internet.
